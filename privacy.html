Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 — Last Updated: July 1, 2025

Your Privacy, Our Reality

At Bits N Bytes Inc., we value privacy not because it’s trendy — but because we operate without a marketing department breathing down our neck. We don’t harvest your data, we don’t rent it out, and we don’t build behavior profiles. We respect your time, your clicks, and your trust — and we stand by that every single day.

What We Actually Collect

We collect only the basics — whatever your browser naturally leaves behind. This includes general traffic patterns, referral sources, and the kind of technical diagnostics most websites gather (browser version, screen size, maybe your IP — but we’re not chasing you around with cookies).

Frankly, our infrastructure isn’t built for tracking. We’re not running fancy analytics suites or retargeting you with ads. Our stack is lean by design and stays that way.

  • Backend AI & SD Tools — Some experimental features may rely on locally hosted or remote inference endpoints (Stable Diffusion, multimodal LLMs, etc). While we limit logging, models may collect prompts or crash logs for troubleshooting or performance tuning.
  • Cloudflare — Powers our edge compute and DNS stack. May collect traffic metrics, bot patterns, or threat fingerprints.
  • Google / Bing — May index or cache pages and gather search behavior tied to our listings.
  • MakeXYZ — Third-party 3D print lead generator. Submissions go directly to their platform.
  • GitHub / Discord — Open-source code and community platforms. They set their own cookie and usage policies.
  • OpenAI or actual open-source AI — Any interaction with our playground chatbots may involve calls to OpenAI or affiliated inference engines or projects, which may log requests for performance or research.
  • Chase (JPMorgan) — Our payment processor for hardware sales. They may collect transactional data as required by law and financial policy.

How We Protect Your Privacy

Our website is static. No dynamic databases. No user logins. No SaaS stack to phish. We operate on an HTML5-first philosophy — using edge caching, lightweight logic, and browser-native features to deliver function without friction.

Conceptually? We’re about as unhackable as a business can get in the modern web. That said, nothing is impossible — so if something weird ever happens and any of our limited data is breached, we’ll notify affected parties (if any) directly and transparently.

Use Common Sense, Too

While we work to maintain your privacy, we encourage you to follow good digital hygiene. Don’t share sensitive information over chatbot inputs or public forms. Avoid entering passwords, financials, or personal identifiers where they don’t belong.

Final Word

We're not here to stalk you. We’re here to build cool stuff and fix hard problems — and we believe your data should stay yours.
If you want to talk about an Ai-Structured web deployment, just reach out. We love building for folks who value privacy the same way we do.
