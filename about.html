What We’re About

We build lean, mean, green machines.

We growth-hack — not ad-spend.

We create without permission.

We innovate outside the stack.

We question the system — then find ways around it.

We’re not just a computer repair shop. We’re a garage-born R&D lab with solder on the desk and scraped knuckles from pulling server rails. We help fix the world one repaste, one STL, one script at a time. If a tool doesn’t exist, we’ll build it. If a system is broken, we’ll challenge it. And if a kid walks in with a Raspberry Pi and a question — we’ll make time.

Our tech is tactical. Our methods are transparent. And our favorite phrase is: “I bet we could make that work.”

The Formal About

Bits N Bytes Inc. is a Delaware close S-corporation operating from Lorena, Texas. We offer by-appointment services in computer repair, 3D printing, custom hardware builds, AI edge solutions, niche electronics, and experimental tech. Founded on a lean philosophy and built without external funding, we prioritize transparency, privacy, and self-reliance. All work is performed in-house by experienced technicians. We also support schools, low-income households, and open-source communities — not because we have to, but because we want to.

Have a project? Email us or book an appointment.
