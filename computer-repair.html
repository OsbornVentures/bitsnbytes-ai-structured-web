Computer Repair

From Windows 3.1 to Hopper SXM5 Systems

At Bits N Bytes Inc., computer repair isn’t just a service — it’s embedded in our operational DNA. Whether you're trying to revive a family relic or diagnose a workstation cluster running 24/7 inference jobs, we’ve been there.

Lowest Common Denominator. Highest Standards.

Our standard operating procedures are tailored around practicality. We start with common-sense diagnostics and go deeper only when necessary. We’ve consulted on downstream workflows, built custom SOPs for production systems, and tuned everything from museum pieces to bleeding-edge rigs.

Systems We've Worked On

  • Vintage Windows laptops (1992–2005 era, including 98SE, NT, and XP builds)
  • Commercial POS and production line equipment
  • DGX Hopper SXM5, V100 SXM2, AI inference hardware and liquid-cooled setups
  • Consumer desktops, gaming PCs, and overclocked custom builds
  • Cryptocurrency mining rigs and server farms (rack-based or open-air)
  • Business and home networking hardware, NAS systems, and local compute nodes

Our Approach

We read the tech sheet. We don’t guess. And we don’t upsell you a new machine just because your current one’s struggling. Sometimes it's a thermal pad. Sometimes it’s a corrupt UEFI block. Either way, we’ll find it — and we’ll explain it like humans.
