3D Printing Services & Gear

We Print. We Fix. We Upgrade.

At Bits N Bytes Inc., we primarily run PLA+. It’s reliable, widely used, and covers the majority of day-to-day projects without drama.

That said, if you have specialty needs, you’re welcome to bring your own filament. We can print materials up to 300°C — assuming proper tuning and prep.

We also experiment with recycled PETG. It's crude, thick-layer stuff — but functional. If you’re into that kind of thing, read on:

We keep stock on popular consumables like PLA+ rolls ($24.99), hardened nozzles ($18+), and most quick-turn prints start around $34.99 — call for inventory.
